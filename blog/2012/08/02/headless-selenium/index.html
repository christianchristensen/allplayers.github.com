Headless Selenium

Aug 2nd, 2012

Throughout my work here, I (Allan Chappell) have made strides revamping the way we use Selenium here at AllPlayers. In doing so, I have started a small project on GitHub for a headless implementation of a selenium server setup that shows some of how developers here at AllPlayers are not only Open Sourcing our efforts in just the Drupal world, but everywhere we can.

In addition to this, I promote it through my blog. Check it out and feel free to contribute to the efforts of making automated testing easier!
